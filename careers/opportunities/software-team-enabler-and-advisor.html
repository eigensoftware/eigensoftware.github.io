Opportunities

Job Title: Software Team Enabler and Advisor

Work Location: Leatherhead

Salary: Dependant on Experience

Employment Type

  • Full Time
  • Permanent

We are working on new ways of modelling and interacting with information to help Oil and Gas companies become much more efficient.  It's going so well we need someone to help keep the sales team on plant earth and help our amazing team of developers figure out what the priorities are and deliver them in an efficient way.

This role is a unique position and will suit someone with a lot of technical experience but who does not want to go into management or specialise in any particular area.  We need someone who understands all the aspects of the software stack and also project delivery and commercial drivers and can help the developers enjoy their jobs and deliver great code, while at the same time helping the C-suite understand where there are opportunities to be doing things better and how.

The most important things you will bring is a "can do" attitude and solid technical expertise (we don't want any arm waving pie in the sky), but in addition to this you will also be able to show:

  • In depth understanding of the full technical stack and associated version control, build, testing & deployment systems
  • Good people skills
  • Ability to articulate both technical and non-technical ideas
  • Maintain up-to-date knowledge of technology standards, industry trends, emerging technologies, and software development best practices by attending relevant conferences and reading widely
  • Knowledge and practical application of JIRA and Confluence
  • A good Computer Science degree from a recognised University
  • Seek opportunities for innovation – both internally and externally with our clients
  • Excellent communication skills in English; both oral and written
  • Proficient in some scripting and programming languages (Python, Ruby, Perl, PowerShell, Golang, etc.)

Oh, and be prepared to tell us about what you love doing in your spare time.

Just so you know, Eigen is an equal opportunity employer and we value diversity. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, colour, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status or disability status.

We’re also passionate about delivering quality by ensuring the most valuable use of our talents and experience. We aim to support different working patterns to ensure a diverse collective of people can call ThoughtWorks their home so if you’re looking to work with high profile clients, delivering digital transformation and innovation, get in touch and chat to us about working flexibly!

To apply please submit your CV and tell us why you want to join Eigen.
