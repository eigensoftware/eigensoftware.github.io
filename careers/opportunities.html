Job Title: Applications Support Analyst

Work Location: Baku

Salary: Dependant on Experience

Employment Type

  • Full Time
  • Permanent

Duties and Responsibilities:

An enthusiastic and experienced individual is required, able to troubleshoot a broad spectrum of technical issues in situ and to develop robust best-fit solutions to a wide range of application support challenges. Applicant must be self-reliant and self-motivated.

As Application Support Analyst your responsibilities are to:

  • liaise directly with senior stakeholders to provide a high productivity and high quality service to meet the needs of demanding end users. You will talk to users on a daily basis and will need to have excellent technical understanding in order to provide the best, most effective solutions.
  • own and manage incidents by liaising with the development teams, tracking failures, organizing meetings where necessary and following up, post incident.
  • identify and build tools and scripts in order to enable smoother day to day running of the business, enhancing and advancing technical output for the business.

  • Operation of IT Service Delivery and Service Desk systems eg SLAs, Allocation of tickets, performance monitoring.

    • Database Management Systems (MS SQL Server)
    • Citrix, FTP, WSUS, Anti virus repository servers
  • Able to go on 24/7 support rota and carry on-call telephone 1 week in 4.
  • Perform related duties as assigned by supervisor.
  • Maintain compliance with all company policies and procedures.

Education and/or Work Experience Requirements:

Essential

  • Experience of Application Support, particularly:
    • Java EE/Tomcat/Apache
    • Linux and VMWare platform
    • Scripting (Python, VBA etc)
    • Web development (HTML, CSS, Javascript, JSON)
    • FTP/SFTP
    • SQL and Relational databases (MS SQL Server, PostgreSQL)
    • REST API and Web services
    • Version control systems (GIT)
    • Build tools
  • University degree/Diploma in Computing or related subject
  • Familiarity with Cybersecurity standards eg ISA 99/ANSI/ISA-62443 suite
  • Familiarity with Networks, routing and firewalls
  • Excellent English verbal and written communication skills, including ability to effectively communicate with internal and external customers
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills

Desirable

  • Microsoft Certifications
  • Cisco Certifications
  • Familiarity with data transfer protocols eg OPC/DCOM, Modbus, Profibus
  • Familiarity with Real Time Historian and Alarm Management systems, particularly Aspentech IP.21, OSIsoft PI, Honeywell Alarm Manager/Dynamo
