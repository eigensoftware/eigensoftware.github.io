Job Title: Senior Java Developer

Work Location: Leatherhead

Salary: Dependent on Experience

Employment Type

  • Full Time
  • Permanent

At Eigen we are working on new ways of modelling and interacting with information to help Oil and Gas companies become much more efficient.  We deal with all kinds of data from live sensors to report and documents and one of the biggest challenges is helping companies replace spreadsheets!  Every customer is unique and one of the biggest challenges we have around the back end is to ensure the platform can scale while still being flexible and supportable.  We build data models that describe how all the information is connected so that we can build more intelligent systems that understand context.  Our team has done an amazing job so far and we are looking for another tenacious technical problem solver to join our team as we grow.

We need someone with a passion for technology and an eagerness to learn.  This role is ideally suited to someone with a couple (2+) of years experience who is looking to develop their skills and take on more responsibility under the guidance of our Lead Software Architect.  You will be able to show us some examples of some excellent Java code you've written over the last 2 years.

You will definitely need:

  • To show you have a working knowledge of Java 8 + Spring
  • Some experience building Linux systems (RHEL, Ubuntu, CentOS, etc.)
  • Unrestricted right to work in the UK
  • a good Computer Science, Engineering, Maths or Physics degree from a recognised University

Although not essential, we would love for you to have things like a knowledge of the web from top-to-bottom, a good Computer Science, Engineering, Maths or Physics degree from a recognised University.  A working knowledge of scripting and programming languages such as Python, Ruby, Perl, PowerShell, Golang, etc. is helpfull and some experience with unit testing and automated testing tools.  If you have experience with Graph Databases (e.g. Neo4j), REST APIs (e.g. ElasticSearch), GraphQL, Kubernetes and related technologies that's also a bonus.

Oh, and be prepared to tell us about what you love doing in your spare time.

Just so you know, Eigen is an equal opportunity employer and we value diversity. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, colour, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status or disability status.

We’re also passionate about delivering quality by ensuring the most valuable use of our talents and experience. We aim to support different working patterns to ensure a diverse collective of people can call ThoughtWorks their home so if you’re looking to work with high profile clients, delivering digital transformation and innovation, get in touch and chat to us about working flexibly!
