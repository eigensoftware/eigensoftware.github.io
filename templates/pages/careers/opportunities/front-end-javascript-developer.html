Job Title: Front End Javascript Developer

Work Location: Leatherhead

Salary: Dependant on Experience

Employment Type

  • Full Time
  • Permanent

We need someone with a great eye for design and a "can do" attitude to Front End development combined with solid technical ability.  We build awesome looking UIs for industrial systems and they get used by very demanding customers so they've got to look good and keep working.

We deal with all kinds of data from live sensors to report and documents and one of the biggest challenges is helping companies replace spreadsheets! Every customer is unique and we need to make sure the platform can scale while still being flexible and supportable.  We build data models that describe how all the information is connected so that we can build more intelligent systems that understand context.  Our team has done an amazing job so far and we are looking for another tenacious technical problem solver to join our team as we grow.

Our ideal candidate will have a proven track record of creating, maintaining and delivering excellent code in an agile environment.  The role is primarily JavaScript but demonstrable knowledge of the full stack is an advantage.  In this role, it is important to be able to iterate rapidly based on user feedback, experiment with different ideas, without losing sight of engineering quality. You’ll be willing to share your opinion on technical direction, and contribute to the mix of ideas.

You will definitely need:

  • Significant JavaScript experience
  • Experience with ES2015 (ES6)
  • Experience with React
  • HTML / CSS / SASS
  • Excellent communication skills in English; both oral and written

Although not essential, we would love for you to have:

  • A good Computer Science, Engineering, Maths or Physics degree from a recognised University

  • Experience with GIT
  • Experience with continuous integration tools (Jenkins, Travis-CI, GoCD)
  • Experience with Confluence & JIRA
  • An interesting hobby

Oh, and be prepared to tell us about what you love doing in your spare time.

Just so you know, Eigen is an equal opportunity employer and we value diversity. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, colour, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status or disability status.

We’re also passionate about delivering quality by ensuring the most valuable use of our talents and experience. We aim to support different working patterns to ensure a diverse collective of people can call ThoughtWorks their home so if you’re looking to work with high profile clients, delivering digital transformation and innovation, get in touch and chat to us about working flexibly!

To apply please submit your CV and tell us why you want to join Eigen.
