Barrier Status

Eigen Ingenuity can model the complex nature of Safety Barriers (LOPA, HAZOP etc.) on an industrial asset and display aggregated status in an easy to understand way. The Shared Asset Model is used to model the Barrier Functions, Codes and Elements and relate them to the physical assets (such as valves and instruments) as well as logical groupings (e.g. Area or System), as well as Safety Instrumented Functions (SIFs) and control loops.

New smart ways of visualising data can be used to help users take in a lot of information in one glance and draw attention to problems.

Barrier element criticality and other factors can be modelled and status calculated according to specific requirements.

See how Eigen Ingenuity helped a new Asset in the arctic keep on top of critical safety information.
