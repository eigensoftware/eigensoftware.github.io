Shared Awareness

Beautiful dashboards and intelligent new ways of displaying data combined with the access to data in the Eigen Ingenuity platform make it easy to create dashboards and displays that show the live status of your operation.

Keep everyone informed with minimum effort and help everyone to feel part of the same team. Eigen Ingenuity enables shared awareness of key information to improve visibility of status across disciplines and locations.

Eigen Ingenuity support lean operations by:

  • reducing meeting time/less time in meetings
  • reducing time spent looking for information
  • reducing time spend understanding problems
  • less time in administration
  • automatic distribution of information
