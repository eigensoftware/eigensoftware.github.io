Connect

Eigen has the software, hardware, and engineering expertise to get your data connected and useful. From existing data source such as OSIsoft PI, SAP, Aveva or Tieto through to new custom applications for analytics.

Visualise

Transform your operations and take advantage of a new connected way of working. Reduce the complexity of running your business and get everything you need through a single portal. Eigen Ingenuity can be deployed quickly and scales with you. Elegant dashboards let everyone see what is going on. Combined information brings new insights.

Transform

Users don't need to go into multiple systems any more to understand what is going on. Eigen Ingenuity is built around Eigen's revolutionary data model that lets you view your data in new ways. Turn data and relationships into actionable insights.

Quickly built new applications and automate workflows using the open APIs and extensive library of interactive web components. Integrated search and alerting enable users to have their attention focused on what matters.

Future

Eigen Ingenuity is constantly being expanded with new functionality and the latest technologies for visualisation & analytics.
